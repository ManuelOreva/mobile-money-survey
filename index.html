About Recon Edge

We don’t surface data.
We surface decisions.

Recon Edge is a data analytics and research firm built for enterprises operating at the edge of market complexity. We integrate structured and unstructured data at scale, then translate it into forward-looking intelligence your leadership can act on — now.

Our teams embed directly into your operational stack. Not as consultants — as analysts. We stay until the insight is embedded in how you work.
